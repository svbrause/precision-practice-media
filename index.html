How It Works

We meet patients where
they are — and walk them
all the way to your door.

1
Meet them where they are
Patients dealing with chronic joint pain, BPH, or fibroids are searching for answers on their devices — late at night, after another failed injection, or following a surgery consult they didn't like. We build condition-specific, medically credible patient brands that appear when and where those searches happen, positioning minimally invasive treatment as the alternative they didn't know existed.
2
Educate and qualify
Our patient-facing brands don't just capture interest — they educate. Patients learn about their condition, understand what minimally invasive procedures can offer, and complete a structured intake assessment. Our clinical coordination team reviews every submission and screens against documented candidacy criteria. Patients who don't qualify are not forwarded — no exceptions.
3
Verify and book
Qualified candidates have their insurance eligibility confirmed before a single appointment is made. Our team handles all outreach, follow-up, and scheduling directly into your calendar. By the time a patient appears on your books, the clinical and financial groundwork is already done.

You focus on the procedure. We handle everything that comes before it.

Our Foundation
"The clinical side was never the hard part. The infrastructure to find, qualify, and convert patients — reliably, at volume — was what didn't exist."

Precision Practice Media was founded by interventional radiologists who had built independent practices and recognized a gap that no marketing company was equipped to fill. We built the solution ourselves.

That background informs everything: the qualification criteria, the patient-facing brands built to clinical standards, and the coordination team that understands what it means to have a physician's professional reputation attached to every patient interaction.

We also understand what it means to operate as an independent practice — and what it takes to build one that grows on your terms, not a hospital system's schedule.

Patient Data & HIPAA Compliance
Our intake and qualification process is built to HIPAA standards. Patient data is never shared, sold, or used outside of your network relationship. All coordination staff operate under BAA-governed agreements. We apply the same standard to patient data that you hold yourself to in the clinic.
